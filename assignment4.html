Importance of Sports

Sports have been a part of human civilization for centuries, and for good reason. Engaging in sports activities not only provides physical benefits, but it also has a profound impact on our mental and emotional well-being. One of the main reasons why sports make us feel good is the release of endorphins in our body. These chemicals, often referred to as 'happy hormones,' are released when we exercise, giving us a sense of euphoria and happiness.
